Fact 1: The Amazing Octopus

Octopuses have three heart nine brains and blue blood. Two hearts pump blood to the grills,while the third pumps it to the rest of the body.

How smart are octopuses?

Octopuses can open jars, solve puzzles, and even recognise human faces. Scientists consider them one of the most intelligent invetebrates on Earth.

Fact 2: Lightning Strikes

Lightning strikes the Earth about 100 times every single second. That is roughly 8 million lightning strikes per day!

How hot is lightning?

Asingle bolt of lightning is around 30,000 Kelvin - five times hotter than the surface of the sun.

Can lightning strike twice?

Yes! absolutely can and does strike the same place more than once, especially tall structures like towers and trees.

Fact 3: The Deep Ocean

The deepest point on Earth is the Marina Trench, which reaches a depth of about 11 kilometres-deep enough to fit Mount Everest inside with room to spare!

Fun comparison

If you placed Mount Everest at the bottom of the Mariana Trench,its peak would still be more than two kilometres underwater.
